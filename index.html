Marvel Crossover

Select any 2 affiliations from this list: X-Men, Avengers, Guardians of the Galaxy, Deadpool. Build your team with 4 cards from each of those affilations.

  • If UXM Professor Xavier is on your team, you may only have 3 X-Men cards; for a total team of 7 cards.

Roll For It

Roll a 20 sided die twice, and select two sets from the following list. The organizer may roll for a third set, if desired.

If the die rolls higher than sets exist for, reroll the die.

  1. Any team pack, draft pack, or starter pack without an associated gravity feed product.
  2. AVX
  3. UXM
  4. ...
  • The organizer is encouraged to ban selected "overpowered" cards from the selected sets, such as Tsarina, Lantern Ring or Beholder.

Red Shirts

Dice must be red. If a swirly die, the main color must be red. Ink doesn't count.

  • If a player needs to know whether a certain die is allowed, they should contact the organizer in advance of the event, or come with a backup plan.

Go Sounders!

Dice must be green or blue. If a swirly die, the main color must be blue or green. Ink doesn't count.

  • If a player needs to know whether a certain die is allowed, they should contact the organizer in advance of the event, or come with a backup plan.

Black As the Night

Dice must be black or dark grey. If a swirly die, the main color must be black or dark grey. Ink doesn't count.

  • If a player needs to know whether a certain die is allowed, they should contact the organizer in advance of the event, or be prepared with a backup plan.

Ladies' Night

All characters must be women or girls.

  • No actions are allowed on the 8 card team. Basic Actions are allowed as normal.
  • Art on the card will be used to determine gender. For example, the OP "Loki - Loki Laufeyson" card would be allowed in this event.
  • If a player needs to know whether a certain character is allowed, they should contact the organizer in advance of the event, or be prepared with a backup plan.

Gentlemen's Night

All characters must be men or boys.

  • No actions are allowed on the 8 card team. Basic Actions are allowed as normal.
  • Art on the card will be used to determine gender. For example, the common "Loki - Loki Laufeyson" card would be allowed in this event.
  • If a player needs to know whether a certain character is allowed, they should contact the organizer in advance of the event, or be prepared with a backup plan.
