Marvel Crossover

Select any 2 affiliations from this list: X-Men, Avengers, Guardians of the Galaxy, Deadpool. Build your team with 4 cards from each of those affilations.

  • If UXM Professor Xavior is on your team, you may only have 3 X-Men cards; for a total team of 7 cards.

Roll For It

Roll a 20 sided die twice, and select two sets from the following list. The organizer may roll for a third set, if desired.

If the die rolls higher than sets exist for, reroll the die.

  1. Any team pack, draft pack, or starter pack without an associated gravity feed product.
  2. AVX
  3. UXM
  4. ...
  • The organizer is encouraged to ban over-powered cards from the selected sets.
