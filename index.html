Dice Master Event Ideas

A collection of constructed event themes for organizers to use in their Dice Masters events.

Suggested rules will help prevent devastating or over-used strategies from appearing in player's teams.

Unless explicitly stated otherwise, all Wizkids Banned Cards are banned from these events.

Players should construct a team with up to 8 cards (characters or actions) and no more than 20 dice, and 2 basic actions using one of these themes. Cards and dice must be from Dice Masters products such as starters, team packs, gravity feeds or OP promotions.

Marvel X-Over

Select any 2 affiliations from X-Men, Avengers, Guardians of the Galaxy, Spider-Friends, Deadpool, S.H.I.E.L.D., New Warriors, Thunderbolts, Sinister Six, Mystics, Marvel Knights or Stark Industries. Build your team with 4 cards from each of those affiliations.

  • If UXM Professor Xavier is on your team, you may only have 3 X-Men cards; for a total team of 7 cards.
  • Any globals which add or remove the Villain affiliation are considered blank.

DC Crossover

Select any 2 affiliations from Justice League, Justice Society of America, Green Lanterns, Red Lanterns, Legion of Doom, Teen Titans, Batman Family, Team Superman, or Crime Syndicate. Build your team with 4 cards from each of those affiliations.

  • Flip cards are allowed, but the player must start with the legal affiliation side up, and may not flip the cards until their second turn.
  • Any globals which add or remove the Villain affiliation are considered blank.
  • Black Manta - Deep Sea Deviant is not allowed in this event.

Holy Rollers

All cards must have a good alignment, or one of the following affiliations: Avengers, Fantastic Four, Phoenix Force, X-Men, Guardians of the Galaxy, S.H.I.E.L.D., Spider-Friends, New Warriors, Mystics, Stark Industries, Defenders, Inhumans, Justice League, Justice Society of America, Teen Titans, Blue Lanterns, Green Lanterns, Indigo Tribe, Orange Lanterns, White Lanterns, Star Sapphire Corps, Teen Titans, Batman Family, Team Superman, Team Arrow, New Gods, Marvel Knights or Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles.

  • Any globals which add or remove the Villain affiliation are considered blank.
  • Flip cards are allowed, but the player must start with the legal affiliation side up, and may not flip the cards until their second turn.

Only the Good Die Young

All cards must have the Villain affiliation or have an evil alignment.

  • Any globals which add or remove the Villain affiliation are considered blank.
  • Flip cards are allowed, but the player must start with the legal affiliation side up, and may not flip the cards until their second turn.
  • Villainous Pact is not allowed in this event.

Titles

All characters must have a title in their name or sub-title text - such as "Professor", "Dr.", "Doctor", "Mr.", "Ms.", "Queen", "King", "Lord" or "Lady".

  • No actions are allowed on the 8 card team. Basic Actions are allowed as normal.

Roll For It

Roll a 20 sided die twice; those rolls determine two sets to build from based on the following list. The organizer may roll for a third set, if desired.

If the die rolls a number higher than sets exist for, re-roll the die.

  1. Any team pack, draft pack, or starter set without an associated gravity feed product.
  2. Avergers vs. X-Men (Marvel)
  3. Uncanny X-Men (Marvel)
  4. Yu-Gi-Oh
  5. Battle for Faerûn (Dungeons & Dragons)
  6. Justice League (DC)
  7. Age of Ultron (Marvel)
  8. War of Light (DC)
  9. Amazing Spider-Man (Marvel)
  10. Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles Box Set 1 (TMNT)
  11. World's Finest (DC)
  12. Civil War (Marvel)
  13. Faerûn Under Siege (Dungeons & Dragons)
  14. Green Arrow and The Flash (DC)
  15. Deadpool (Marvel)
  16. Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles Heroes in a Half Shell Box Set (TMNT)
  17. Batman (DC)
  18. Guardians of the Galaxy (Marvel)
  • Ban certain "overpowered" cards from the selected sets, such as Tsarina, Lantern Ring or Dwarf Wizard.
  • If local players don't like a certain set (such as AVX or Yu-Gi-Oh), re-roll the die if that set is rolled.

Red Shirts

Dice must be red or pink. (Ink doesn't count.) If it is a marbled die, the coloring on the corresponding card must be depicted as mainly red or pink.

  • If a player needs to know whether a certain die is allowed, they should contact the organizer in advance of the event, or come with a backup plan.

Go Sounders (or Seahawks)!

Dice must be blue or green. (Ink doesn't count.) If it is a marbled die, the coloring on the corresponding card must be depicted as mainly blue or green.

  • If a player needs to know whether a certain die is allowed, they should contact the organizer in advance of the event, or come with a backup plan.

Black As the Night

Dice must be black or dark grey. (Ink doesn't count.) If it is a marbled die, the coloring on the corresponding card must be depicted as mainly black or dark grey.

  • If a player needs to know whether a certain die is allowed, they should contact the organizer in advance of the event, or be prepared with a backup plan.

Glass Houses

Dice must be clear or translucent; I.E., when held in front of a light source, light must show through the die.

  • If a player needs to know whether a certain die is allowed, they should contact the organizer in advance of the event, or be prepared with a backup plan.

Ladies' Night

All characters must be women or girls.

  • No actions are allowed on the 8 card team. Basic Actions are allowed as normal.
  • Art on the card will be used to determine gender. For example, the OP "Loki - Loki Laufeyson" card would be allowed in this event.
  • If a player needs to know whether a certain character is allowed, they should contact the organizer in advance of the event, or be prepared with a backup plan.

Boys' Club

All characters must be men or boys.

  • No actions are allowed on the 8 card team. Basic Actions are allowed as normal.
  • Art on the card will be used to determine gender. For example, the common "Loki - Loki Laufeyson" card would be allowed in this event.
  • If a player needs to know whether a certain character is allowed, they should contact the organizer in advance of the event, or be prepared with a backup plan.

Golden Age Marvel

Build the team using any combination of cards from Marvel sets or OP.

  • These cards are not allowed in this event: Black Widow - Tsarina, Green Goblin - Gobby.
  • All Basic Actions must be from Marvel sets.

Modern Age Marvel

Build the team using any combination of cards from Modern Age Marvel sets.

  • All Basic Actions must be from Modern Age Marvel sets.

Golden Age DC

Build the team using any combination of cards from DC sets or OP.

  • Guy Gardener - Blinding Rage is not allowed in this event.
  • All Basic Actions must be from DC sets.

Modern Age DC

Build the team using any combination of cards from Modern Age DC sets.

  • Guy Gardener - Blinding Rage is not allowed in this event.
  • All Basic Actions must be from Modern Age DC sets.

Dungeons & Dragons

Build your team using any combination of cards from Dungeons & Dragons sets or OP.

  • All Basic Actions must be from Dungeons & Dragons sets.

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles

Build your team using any combination of cards from Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles sets.

  • All Basic Actions must be from Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles sets.

Rainbow Warriors

All characters must have a color in their name or sub-title text. Examples: Black Panther, Scarlet Witch, White or Black Lanterns.

  • No actions are allowed on the 8 card team. Basic Actions are allowed as normal.

Animal Style

All characters must have an animal in their name or sub-title text, or have the Monster affiliation from D&D. Examples: Beast, Beast Boy, Blue Dragon, Ace the Bat Hound, Wolverine and Dex-Starr - Rage Kitty are allowed at this event. Miguel O'Hara - Spider-Man 2099 is allowed, but Miguel O'Hara - Lyrate Lifeform Approximation is not.

  • No actions are allowed on the 8 card team. Basic Actions are allowed as normal.
  • Batwoman (any sub-title) is not allowed in this event.
